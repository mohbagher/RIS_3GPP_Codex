  WHY ACCURACY DROPS WITH MORE PROBES AT HIGH SPEED:
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Each probe costs 2.5µs. Total probing time = M × 2.5µs.
  Channel correlation at data phase: ρ = max(|J₀(2πf_D·M·T_slot)|, 0.15)
  When ρ < 0.5, probes describe a STALE channel → beam selection fails.

  TIMING CONSTRAINT: Probing time must not exceed coherence time.
  When M·T_slot > T_c, probes themselves span multiple coherence intervals,
  making measurements internally inconsistent. Such M values are marked ✗.
